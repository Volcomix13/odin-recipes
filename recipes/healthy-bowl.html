Healthy Bowl

Description

Healthy vegan bowl loaded with hearty vegetables, topped with hummus, and a touch of lime juice to bring everything together.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 sweet potato, cut into 3/4" cubes
  • 2 large carrots, slices
  • 1 1/2 brussel sprouts, half or quartered
  • 1 1/2 broccoli florets
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • salt and pepper, to taste

To serve

  • 1-2 avocados
  • big dollop of hummus

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Prepare vegetables: remove any unsightly spots on the sweet potato skins before cubing, peel the carrots if needed and half or quarter the brussels sprouts depending on the size.
  3. Place vegetables, chickpeas and lemon in a large roasting pan or rimmed baking sheet.
  4. Toss with oil and sprinkle with salt & pepper to taste.
  5. Roast for 40-45 minutes, stirring halfway through. Vegetables should be slightly browned on the edges and fork tender.
  6. Remove from oven and let cool a few minutes.
  7. In individual bowls, serve vegetables with 1/3 cup quinoa, handful of spinach, sliced avocado and a nice dollop of hummus. Top with a sprinkle of salt and and pepper to taste and a squeeze of roasted lemons.
